Catalog Photos — Google Merchant Center
Quality standard and compliance requirements

Google's Mandatory Rules

RequirementSpecification
BackgroundPure white (#FFFFFF) or real-use context
Minimum resolution800 × 800 px (recommended)
FormatJPG or PNG
WatermarksNot allowed
Overlaid textNot allowed
Decorative bordersNot allowed

Required Shots per Product

Each product in the catalog must have at least the following photo types, in order of priority.
  • Main image — product on white background, centered, occupying at least 75% of the frame
  • Left and right side views — to show depth and structure
  • Detail shot — focus on joint, connection, or key component
  • In-use context — product installed in a real environment (warehouse, depot)

Pre-Upload Checklist

  • White background or real-use environment
  • Product occupies at least 75% of the frame
  • No text, logo, or watermark on the image
  • Minimum resolution of 800 × 800 px
  • Descriptive file name — e.g. cantilever-rack-96in-single.jpg not IMG_0034.jpg
