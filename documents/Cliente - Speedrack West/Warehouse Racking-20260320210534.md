# Warehouse Racking

## Selective Pallet Rack
[https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-racking/pallet-rack](https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-racking/pallet-rack)
Selective pallet racking is the most common, versatile warehouse storage system, using uprights and beams for direct, easy access to every pallet via forklift, making it ideal for high SKU counts, fast turnover, and mixed product types, offering a cost-effective way to utilize vertical space by placing pallets in single or double rows with adjustable beam levels.

## Cantilever Rack
[https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-racking/cantilever-rack](https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-racking/cantilever-rack)
A specialized storage system featuring horizontal arms extending from vertical columns, designed to hold long, bulky, or oddly shaped items like pipes, lumber, furniture, and steel without front uprights obstructing access, allowing for easy front loading and efficient use of space for materials that don't fit standard pallet racks.

## Pallet Flow Rack
[https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-racking/p/pallet-flow-rack](https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-racking/p/pallet-flow-rack)
A high-density, gravity-fed storage system using sloped roller lanes where pallets loaded at the back automatically roll to the front for picking, enabling efficient First-In, First-Out (FIFO) inventory management, perfect for fast-moving goods like food or pharmaceuticals. This dynamic system saves space by reducing aisles, keeps the newest stock at the rear while presenting older stock at the front, and minimizes forklift travel, boosting warehouse throughput. 
![](https://t9011829912.p.clickup-attachments.com/t9011829912/1b06eefb-82ff-4e0a-a148-4ef32f838b51/Gravity%20Flow%20Pallet%20Rack.png)

## **Pushback Rack**
[https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-racking/p/pushback-rack](https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-racking/p/pushback-rack)
A high-density warehouse storage system using inclined lanes with carts or shuttles to store pallets in a **Last-In, First-Out (LIFO)** (LIFO) flow, allowing for deep storage (2-6 pallets) from a single aisle, maximizing space while offering better accessibility than drive-in racks. Pallets are pushed back into the lane by subsequent loads, and gravity brings them forward for unloading. 
![](https://t9011829912.p.clickup-attachments.com/t9011829912/074ebbea-b068-496b-9d70-c926b7c5f1c7/Push%20back%20racks.png)

## **Drive-In Rack**
[https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-racking/p/drive-in-rack](https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-racking/p/drive-in-rack)
A high-density pallet racking system that maximizes warehouse space by eliminating aisles, allowing forklifts to drive _into_ the rack structure to load/unload pallets. It's ideal for storing large quantities of similar products (single SKU) and works on a LIFO (Last-In, First-Out) basis (drive-in), though a similar drive-through version allows FIFO access. 
![](https://t9011829912.p.clickup-attachments.com/t9011829912/ad3b4644-7f81-4b11-8ca9-02b7dd49ebb2/Drive-in.png)
