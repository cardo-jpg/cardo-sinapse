## **Column Guard**
[https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/column-protectors/p/column-guard](https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/column-protectors/p/column-guard)
A Column Guard is a protective steel barrier designed to shield pallet rack uprights from forklift collisions, which can cause structural damage or system collapse. These guards bolt onto the floor (hardware included) and absorb impact energy, protecting the rack frame. The standard model is 24 inches high, stackable for increased protection, and offers a cost-effective alternative to replacing damaged uprights.

## **RAMGuard Column Protection**
[https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/column-protectors/p/ramguard-column-protection](https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/column-protectors/p/ramguard-column-protection)
RAMGuard Column Protection is an advanced pallet rack safety guard made from a patented rubber-armored metal (a unique energy-absorbing rubber compound with a steel U-shape insert). Unlike traditional guards that bolt to the floor, RAMGuard snaps directly onto 3-inch wide columns without tools or hardware. It absorbs significantly more impact energy than plastic or steel alternatives, protecting the upright from damage without transferring the shock to the floor anchors. It is 12 inches high and stackable for extended coverage.

## **Pallet Rack Flue Guard**
[https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-safety-products/p/pallet-rack-flue-guard](https://www.speedrackwest.com/shop/warehouse-safety-products/p/pallet-rack-flue-guard)
The Pallet Rack Flue Guard is a safety spacer designed to keep the "flue space" (the vertical gap between racks) clear, ensuring compliance with fire codes like NFPA13 and IBC 2012. These 5-inch tall metal dividers bolt onto pallet rack beams to prevent pallets from being pushed too far back, which obstructs sprinkler penetration. They feature a durable gray powder-coated finish, pre-punched holes for easy installation (Tek screws included), and slots compatible with wire decking.
