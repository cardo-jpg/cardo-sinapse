# Who we are

**Speedrack West** is a warehouse storage and material handling company.
We design, sell, and install racking, conveyors, and mezzanine systems that help customers use their space better, move product faster, and stay safe and compliant.
We’re not just an e-commerce store — a big part of our work is **project-based**: designing full rack layouts, mezzanines, and custom solutions for real warehouses.
## **Our Purpose**
Speedrack West exists to drive **our clients’** progress forward. At the heart of it, we’re builders. We focus on solving storage and material handling problems so our clients and their companies can scale up. Our clients are doing amazing things — they’re building something great, and we want to help them do it.
## **Core Values**
### **Fun**
_We bring energy, humor, and positivity to the work we do._
*   Celebrate wins (yours and others) in Chat or team calls
*   Keep a light tone with customers when appropriate
*   Add energy to meetings. Don’t be afraid to bring the vibes
*   Find joy in the work; the hustle doesn’t have to be heavy
Positive people close more deals. People want to buy from someone who loves what they do.
### Loyal
_We have each other’s backs. We’re in it for the long haul._
*   Step in when a teammate’s out or overloaded
*   Be honest with customers. Don’t oversell or overpromise
*   Stick with clients beyond the close
*   Own your deals, own your calendar, own your results
Loyalty is built on consistency and follow-through. Be that person.
### Good People
_We do the right thing, even when no one’s looking._
*   Admit mistakes and fix them fast
*   Show respect across every role and level
*   Be honest, humble, and ethical — always
*   Think long-term reputation, not short-term gain
_Be the marketer that customers trust and teammates love working with._
### Problem Solvers
_We don’t stop at problems. We find the answer and make it happen._
*   Ask better questions to uncover real needs
*   Look around corners; anticipate delays, site issues, or spec mismatches
*   Bring solutions, not complaints
*   Collaborate early with Ops, Engineering, and Support
_Don’t stop at no — figure it out. That’s how we win._
