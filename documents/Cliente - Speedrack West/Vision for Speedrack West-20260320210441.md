### Marketing Strategy
**Target Market / “The List”:**
Our ideal customer is a mid-to-large enterprise ($50M–$1B+ revenue) in the manufacturing, construction supply, or heavy materials distribution industry (e.g., steel fabrication, building materials distribution, lumber products) located primarily west of the Rocky Mountains (e.g., Oregon, Washington, California). They require heavy-duty pallet racking, cantilever racking, push back racking, wire or steel decking, safety products, and related services (installation, permitting) to store large, heavy, or bulky items in their warehouses or yards. Their typical project budget is $50K–$200K, and they value trust, safety compliance, fast delivery, and reliable inventory availability. Decision-makers are operations managers, warehouse managers, or procurement teams who prioritize safety, durability, and speed over the lowest price. They may have established supplier relationships, require competitive bidding, and expect proven reliability before switching vendors.
